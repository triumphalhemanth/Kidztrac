  • Faqs
  • Q1: What is Kidztrac?

    A: Kidztrac is a cloud-based platform designed to manage daycare centers, offering features like activity tracking, automated billing, and a mobile app for parents.

  • Q2: How can I sign up?

    A: Visit our website and click on the "Sign Up" button. Follow the instructions to create an account.

  • Q3: Is there a mobile app?

    A: Yes, Kidztrac offers a mobile app available on both iOS and Android for parents to stay updated on their child's activities.

  • Q4: How do I contact support?

    A: You can contact our support team through the "Contact Us" page on our website or by emailing support@kidztrac.com.

  • Q5: What are the subscription plans?

    A: We offer various subscription plans tailored to the needs of different daycare centers. Visit our "Pricing" page for more details.

  • Q6: Can I track my child's activities in real-time?

    A: Yes, the Kidztrac mobile app allows parents to view real-time updates on their child's activities throughout the day.

  • Q7: How secure is my data with Kidztrac?

    A: We prioritize your privacy and use advanced security measures to protect your data. For detailed information, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

  • Q8: Does Kidztrac support automated billing?

    A: Yes, Kidztrac offers automated billing features to streamline payment processing for daycare centers.

  • Q9: Can I customize the features of Kidztrac for my daycare center?

    A: Yes, Kidztrac offers customizable options to suit the specific needs of your daycare center.

  • Q10: Is there a free trial available?
